Best Studying Techniques for students

There are different effective techniques and methods for students to optimize their learning habbits.

Five best studying techniques:

  • Spaced Repition
  • Active Recall
  • Feyman technique
  • Pomodoro Study Method
  • Leitner System

1.Spaced Repition

Spaced Repition, sometimes often called spaced practice,interleaved,is a study method that involves separatng your study sessions into spaced intervals.It's a simple concept but a game-changer to most students because of how powerfull it is.

2.Active Recall

Active recall is one of the most powerful study methods in existence, and it has a cult-like following among medical school students. Anki and SuperMemo are two of the most widely used digital tools for active recall

3.Feyman technique

The Feynman Technique, developed by Richard Feynman, is a study method for students to learn through the act of teaching. To use the technique, students choose a topic and then explain it in their own words as if teaching a sixth-grader. Then, they improve their explanations and go through the process again until they have mastered the topic.

4.Pomodoro Study Method

The Pomodoro Technique is useful if you get distracted while working on a project or want to understand how long a task takes. It’s ideal for many types of work including writing, coding, design, and study.

5.Leitner System

The Leitner system is based on a series of three flashcard boxes. Box number 1 is where all cards go by default. Then, once you start reviewing the flashcards, you’ll also start changing their placement depending on whether you were able to answer the information written on the card
